Meet Our Founder

Founder & Creator

Quentin Reynolds

Creator of GameHub | Gaming Visionary

Quentin is a visionary in the gaming industry with over 20 years of experience. His passion for interactive entertainment began in the early 90s with classic arcade games. In 2008, he single-handedly created GameHub with a mission to build a centralized platform where gamers of all types could discover, play, and connect over the best games available.

As the sole creator of GameHub, Quentin designed, developed, and launched the platform in 2008. His dedication and innovative approach have transformed GameHub from a personal project into one of the most recognized gaming platforms worldwide, with over 10 million active users and partnerships with all major game developers.

Created from the Ground Up

Quentin personally designed and coded the original GameHub platform in 2008, creating every aspect from the game database to the user interface.
